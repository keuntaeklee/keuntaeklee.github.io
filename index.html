Keuntaek Lee

keuntaek.lee@outlook.com

Hi, my name is Keuntaek Lee and I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon. I did my Ph.D. in the fields of machine learning and deep learning and I was advised by Dr. Evangelos A. Theodorou at Georgia Tech. My research interests include optimization, probabilistic deep learning, reinforcement learning, control theory, and computer vision.

During my Ph.D. studies, I was lucky to work on interesting and challenging research projects including the AutoRally and the AlphaPilot, developing safe ML/DL algorithms for motion planning, decision making, control, and behavior prediction of autonomous vehicles and robots.

I also worked at Honda Research Institute (HRI)-US as a research intern, in the ML-based autonomous driving team.

Before joining Georgia Tech, I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering with the highest honors from Hanyang University, Seoul, Korea.
