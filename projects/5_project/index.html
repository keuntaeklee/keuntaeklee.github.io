The Challenge

The AlphaPilot challenge tests teams of up to 10 participants on their ability to design an AI framework - powered by the NVIDIA Xavier GPU for autonomous systems - capable of flying a drone through three-dimensional race courses using only machine vision and guidance, navigation, and control algorithms. Teams will not have prior knowledge of the exact course layouts, nor will they benefit from GPS, data off-boarding or human intervention of any kind. Designed by DRL, the autonomous drone will provide the sensing, agility and speed needed for competitive racing as part of DRLâ€™s new Artificial Intelligence Robotic Racing (AIRR) Circuit, starting in fall 2019.
